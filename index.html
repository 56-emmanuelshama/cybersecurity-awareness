Welcome to Cybersecurity Awareness

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks.

Cyber world is a digital environment where people interact using technology





Cybersecurity Roles/ cyber actors

A cyber actor is anyone who uses digital systems to carry out actions that affect security , either positively or negatively.

WIZARDS

wizards are the builders of digital system

HEREOS

heroes are cybersecurity professionals who protect who protect systems and data from cyberattacks

CRIMINALS

criminals are the attackers who use cyberattacks to steal data, cause damage, or disrupt services

Protecting Information and Data

Organizations and individuals store sensitive data , cybersecurity ensures CONFIDENTIALITY, INTEGRITY AND AVAILABILITY.

CONFIDENTIALITY

confidentiality is the principle of keeping information private and secure from unauthorized access

INTEGRITY

integrity is the principle of ensuring that information is accurate, complete, and unaltered

AVAILABILITY

availability is the principle of ensuring that information and systems are accessible and usable when needed
